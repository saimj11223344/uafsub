Agriculture University faisalAbad

The University Of Agriculture Faisalabad (UAF) is one of the leading agricultural universities in Pakistan. The main campus of UAF is located in Faisalabad, which is the third-largest city in Pakistan and is known as the "Manchester of Pakistan" due to its thriving textile industry. The University of Agriculture Faisalabad was established in 1906 as the Punjab Agricultural College and Research Institute. Over the years, it has grown into a comprehensive agricultural university offering a wide range of academic programs and research opportunities. UAF offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields related to agriculture, veterinary sciences, animal sciences, food sciences, agribusiness, agricultural engineering, and more. These programs cater to the diverse needs of students interested in pursuing careers in agriculture and allied sciences.Read More

UAF BUREWALA

The UAF sub-campus in Burewala is situated in Burewala city, which is located in the Vehari District of Punjab, Pakistan. The sub-campus offers various academic programs and departments related to agriculture and allied sciences. These may include fields such as agronomy, horticulture, plant breeding and genetics, entomology, plant pathology, agricultural engineering, and more. The specific programs and departments available at the sub-campus may vary. The UAF sub-campus in Burewala provides facilities and resources to support academic and research activities. This may include classrooms, laboratories, research farms, libraries, computer labs, and other necessary infrastructure to facilitate learning and research in the agricultural sciences. Learning Management System

The UAF LMS (Learning Management System) offers a comprehensive platform for students to manage their academic journey. By logging into the system, students can access a range of features and functionalities. One of the primary benefits of the UAF LMS is the ability to check results. Students can view their academic results for different courses and semesters, allowing them to track their progress and evaluate their performance. This feature provides transparency and timely access to their grades. Another valuable feature is the attendance tracking system. The LMS allows students to monitor their attendance records for various classes and sessions.
