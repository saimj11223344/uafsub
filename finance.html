Envision of SAAS

Be an integral part of the educational mission of the UAF Sub campus Burewala by complementing the academic experience and the development of students as lifelong learners by delivering an array of cultural, educational, social, and recreational programs, services, facilities, and leadership development opportunities. Represent the needs, interest and aspirations of the members and students to the University Council, University Officers, Government, the community and other relevant organizations. Educating students in leadership and social responsibility and offering firsthand experiences in global citizenship. Be a student-centered organization that values participatory decision-making. Provide state of the art meeting, gathering, office, community, and study space equal to or better than other excellent institutions. Support a diversified array of programming that reflects the needs of the campus community in order to enhance educational, cultural, and developmental social interaction. Provide a welcoming environment that is the center of campus life, due to its location, programming, and general accessibility to all members of UAF Sub campus Burewala. Serve as a unifying force that honors each individual and values diversity.

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

To act as an apex body to monitor, supervise and guide the students for boosting their skills via organizing seminars, trainings, conferences, workshops, symposia etc. To act as a bridge between students, academia, and administration of the University. To inculcate participatory approach among the students to run different students’ affairs of the university. To provide a platform to the students for Co-curricular activities Sports Community services etc. To develop potential among students for successful career through counselling and skill sharing. To develop linkages with societies of other agricultural teaching institutions for better exposure and healthy competition These aims and objectives shall be pursued without regard to race, sex, sexual orientation, disability, ethnic origin, religion or creed, as well as any form of discrimination

MEMBERSHIP

Student members shall have a cumulative GPA of 2.5 upon appointment to SAAS and must maintain a minimum cumulative GPA of 2.0 during their term of office. They must also be in good standing with the University and shall not be found guilty of violations of the Code of Student Responsibility or Code of Academic Integrity. Members must be able to serve a one year term beginning in August of the term year and be committed to UAF Students’ Council, its mission and vision.
Attendance a. It is expected that each member will attend all regular meetings and participate actively in SAAS. b. Two absences per semester without prior will call for review of continued membership by the Advisory Board. Non-Discrimination a. In keeping with policy of non-discrimination, the Advisory Board as well as the Executive council does not discriminate on the basis of ability/disability, age, culture, ethnicity, gender, language, race, religion, sexual orientation, and socio-economic status. b. It would be mandatory for the registered students of the UAF Sub campus Burewala to register his/her regular membership of SAAS under Office of the Associate Senior Tutor, UAF Sub campus Burewala at the time of admission in any undergraduate/postgraduate degree program and he/she will automatically be registered as non-voting member of the SAAS. c. Seats for voting members of SAAS will be advertised in Burewala campus each year during spring semester. Selection of suitable candidate for President, Senior Vice President, Vice President, General Secretary and Finance Secretary, press secretary and sports secretary will be made on merit basis (Education as well as experience) by Advisory committee constituted by Patron of SAAS Society. Selected members will serve society and will be the voting members of the SAAS for one year.
